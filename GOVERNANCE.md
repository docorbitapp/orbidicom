# Governance

OrbiDICOM is a community open-source project. We keep decisions transparent and lower the
bar for new contributors to become maintainers.

## Roles

- **Contributor** — anyone who opens an issue or PR.
- **Committer** — a contributor with merge rights, earned through a track record of quality
  PRs and reviews. Nominated by a maintainer, confirmed by simple majority of maintainers.
- **Maintainer** — sets direction, owns releases, enforces the Code of Conduct.

## How we work

- Decisions happen in **public** issues and Discussions, not private DMs.
- We aim to triage new issues/PRs within a few days — even a "we won't do this, here's why"
  beats silence.
- Scope changes and roadmap shifts are proposed as issues labeled `proposal` and left open
  for comment before adoption.

## Becoming a committer

Sustained, high-quality contributions (code, reviews, docs, triage) get you nominated. We
grant rights early and generously — distributed ownership is how the project survives any
one person losing interest.
